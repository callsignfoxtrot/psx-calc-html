Discrete Compounding Formulas

Single Payment Series
Compound Amount (F/P, i, n)
\[ F = P (1+i)^n \]

Present Worth (P/F, i, n)
\[ P = F (1+i)^{-n} \]
Equal Payment Series
Compound Amount (F/A, i, n)
\[ F = A \frac{(1+i)^n - 1}{i} \]

Sinking Fund (A/F, i, n)
\[ A = F \frac{i}{(1+i)^n - 1} \]

Present Worth (P/A, i, n)
\[ P = A \frac{(1+i)^n - 1}{i (1+i)^n} \]

Capital Recovery (A/P, i, n)
\[ A = P \frac{i (1+i)^n}{(1+i)^n - 1} \]
Gradient Series
Arithmetic Gradient Conversion (A/G, i, n)
\[ A = G \frac{(1+i)^n - i n - 1}{i ((1+i)^n - 1)} \]
